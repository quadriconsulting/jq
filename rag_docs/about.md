Jeremy Quadri: Professional Summary & Biography
Jeremy Quadri is a senior Offensive and Defensive Security Engineer with over two decades of experience in IT security consulting and management. Based in the UK, his career is marked by a blend of technical mastery and strategic leadership across Europe's top financial and insurance sectors, including Deutsche Bank, RBS Global Markets, AXA Insurance, and HSBC.

Jeremy specializes in helping large enterprises detect and remediate cyber vulnerabilities by designing, developing, and implementing robust information security infrastructures. From his foundational years pioneering penetration testing at Condé Nast to his current focus as a Security Test Lead and Architect, his mission is to drive cybersecurity transformations, optimize IT security investments, and foster a culture of secure, cross-functional innovation.

Core Cybersecurity Philosophy
Holistic DevSecOps: Security must be woven into the fabric of development. Jeremy advocates for shifting security left, building tools and techniques that empower developers rather than blocking them.

Full-Spectrum Defence: True resilience requires both offensive acumen (penetration testing, dynamic analysis) and defensive engineering (Web Application Firewalls, cloud workload protection across AWS and Azure).

Business-Aligned Security: Cybersecurity is not just about finding bugs; it is about managing risk, ensuring compliance with organizational controls, and safeguarding digital assets so the business can thrive safely.
